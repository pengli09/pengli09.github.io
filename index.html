Peng Li is a Research Associate Professor at Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR), Tsinghua University, China. He received his B.S. degree in computer science and technology from Tsinghua University in 2009 and his Ph.D. in computer science and technology from Tsinghua University in 2015. Before joining Tsinghua, he was a principal researcher and team leader at WeChat AI, Tencent. He also previously worked at Institute of Deep Learning (IDL), Baidu Inc. His research interests include artificial intelligence and natural language processing. He received ACL 2023 Outstanding Paper Award and First Prize of Qian Weichang Chinese Information Processing Science and Technology Award.
